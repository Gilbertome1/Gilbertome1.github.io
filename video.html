VIDEO + SOUND Project

DATE | 3/06/18

With this video we follow Hector through a musical journey. While at first the mood is dark as represented by the lack of noise, yet loud static, emphasized background noises, and dull color. The mood changes as colors brighten, the film goes to the outside world, and music fills the empty space. Hector finds a colorful and joyful world once he puts his earphones in and plays his music


Using a Nikon d3400 I filmed my roomate Hector through a process of emotions one feels when stressed, and the relief music brings. I edited the video on premiere and made the music on Garageband, I also used illustrator to create the album cover shown in the video.
